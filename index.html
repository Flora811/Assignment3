    History

    5 Star was launched in India in 1969. In 2016 it was launched in Malaysia and the Philippines in 15g 'mini-bars',
    45g standard bars and 150g sharepacks (of 10 mini-bars). It was launched in Brazil the same year but under
    Cadbury's sister brand Lacta.

    5 Star has also been widely distributed in South Africa since 2017 as a replacement for the defunct Tempo bar.

    In 2019, 5 Star was released in UK shops and supermarkets for a limited time only.
